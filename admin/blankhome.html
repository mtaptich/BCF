#AboutMeQuick

I am a civil engineering Ph.D. student developing a comprehensive understanding of the nexuses shared between civil systems, energy, and our environment. My research focuses on providing methods and tools to enable high resolution life cycle assessments of civil systems (e.g., transportation networks, biofuel production, water distribution, etc.).

I am also a co-founder of the Visualizing Urban Data IdeaLab at UC Berkeley. Check out some of my data visualizations at: vudlab.com/
